The International Space Station

In light of the 50th anniversary of the moon landing, I wanted to post a brief tribute to our current space outpost, the International Space Station. The ISS is one of the most expensive monuments ever built and is a symbol of international cooperation. It is nearing the end of its design life, but given its importance, I doubt it will be allowed to simply fall out of orbit.

It has a captivating view of Earth that NASA shares live with the world. This view from the ISS provides a constant reminder of the importance of protecting our fragile planet.
